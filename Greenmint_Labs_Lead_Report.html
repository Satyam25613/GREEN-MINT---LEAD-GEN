Validation Logic

What makes a lead strong enough for Siddika's outreach.

The selection favors companies with clear signs of scale, AI or workflow complexity, public role alignment, and business functions where Greenmint can credibly improve execution speed and operating leverage.

ICP fit

Growth-stage technology companies with employee, customer, funding, or operating complexity signals. Greenmint's sweet spot is 100-500 employees, $5M-$200M ARR, modern cloud tooling, and documented inefficiency in two or more functions.

Role fit

Targets are operators and functional leaders first: COO, VP People, VP HR, Head of Engineering, CFO, General Counsel, Head of Procurement, or equivalent leaders with direct ownership of bottlenecks and budget.

Problem fit

Each account maps to a practical Greenmint wedge: replacing busywork, preventing revenue leakage, reducing manual risk, speeding hiring, improving procurement control, or giving leaders clearer operational visibility.
